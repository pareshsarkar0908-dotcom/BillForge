Quotation vs estimate: choose the right PDF.

A quotation is usually a firmer proposed price. An estimate is usually an expected price that can change after inspection, scope review, or customer choices.

Create quotation Create estimate
Before invoiceProposal
Firm priceQuotation
Expected priceEstimate
Final billingInvoice

Use a quotation when

  • The scope is clear.
  • You want to present a firm proposed price.
  • The customer needs a formal quote before approving.

Use an estimate when

  • The final cost may change.
  • You need to inspect the job first.
  • Materials, labor, or quantity are still uncertain.
